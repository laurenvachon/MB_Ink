About Us

I was a stay-at-home mom of two teen-aged boys, with a law degree and a love of working with people, when I began to wonder what ELSE was I going to be when I grew up? Then, fate jumped in! As a customer of Lonnie’s, I fell in love with the invitation books, stationery, and all those wonderful personalized gifts! I mentioned that I would be interested in opening a “branch” here in Sudbury, when Lonnie informed me she was ready to retire after THIRTY years in this business....so, I bought Lonnie’s and thus began MB, Ink!

Working with people to help them plan their special occasions has been a joy -- from choosing the perfect invitation, to adding the special touches to their parties, to assisting in shopping for those extra-special personalized gifts, I am having so much fun in this new venture! I feel confident that my honest enthusiasm in this business will help YOU to truly enjoy your shopping experience at MB, Ink!
